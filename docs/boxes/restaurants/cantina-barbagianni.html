Cantina Barbagianni


A small artisan Florentine trattoria in the heart of the city, just a few steps from Palazzo della Nave, Cantina Barbagianni has been offering authentic cuisine with genuine care and passion for more than twenty years.

The atmosphere is warm and intimate — a cozy upstairs dining room and a beautiful cellar room perfect for relaxed dinners. Everything here is homemade: bread, fresh handmade pasta, desserts, and even artisan gelato and sorbets.
Tuscan tradition with creativity, thoughtful vegetarian and vegan options — exactly what a great neighborhood trattoria in Florence should be.



What to Expect


Everything at Cantina Barbagianni is made in-house, from the bread to the fresh handmade pasta prepared daily in their professional in-house laboratory, to the desserts and artisan gelato.
The menu combines Tuscan tradition with creativity, and the kitchen takes real care with vegetarian and vegan options — not an afterthought, but a genuine part of the offer. It's the kind of place people come back to.
