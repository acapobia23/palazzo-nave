Park2Go Florence


Park2Go Florence – The ideal solution for Palazzo Della Nave guests.
Park2Go Florence is located in the heart of Florence's historic centre, at Via Ricasoli 18,
just a 5-minute walk from Palazzo Della Nave in Via Bufalini 3, and 80 metres from the Duomo.
It is a safe, monitored garage with authorised ZTL access and practical, spacious parking bays.

The perfect choice to explore the city on foot, stress-free, with the option to drop off your luggage even before parking.




The garage is located inside Florence's Limited Traffic Zone (ZTL).


Important:

Upon arrival at the garage, staff will electronically register your licence plate with the Municipality of Florence,
authorising ZTL access. The municipal access permit costs €1 and is included in the parking rate.

Once you have entered the ZTL, you have a maximum of 3 hours to reach the garage.
If you exit and re-enter the ZTL during your stay, a new licence plate registration will be required (an additional €1 per each new entry).
The authorisation is valid only for ZTL access and does not cover bus lanes, pedestrian zones, or restricted lanes.
