🍞 Late-Night Bakery in Florence: Panificio Santini

Just a 5-minute walk from Piazza Francesco Ferrucci, there's a legendary spot to satisfy your midnight cravings: Panificio Santini, run by Alfio Santini. A true piece of Florence’s culinary history.

Whether you're heading toward Firenze Sud, coming back from a night out dancing, or simply looking for fresh bread, schiacciata, or pastries for the next day—Alfio is the place to stop.


🕒 Opening Hours

  • Open every night and all day
  • Thursday & Saturday: open from midnight
  • Other nights: open from around 2:30-3:00 AM
  • During the day, you buy from the shop; at night, you buy directly from the bakery lab—a unique experience!

What to Try

  • Chocolate-filled sacchettini - especially when warm, they're indescribably good
  • Pizza al trancio (by the slice) with toppings like:
    • Mozzarella - tomatoes
    • Salsiccia (sausage)
    • Salamino (spicy salami)
  • Cornetti (Italian croissants) with:
    • Custard cream
    • Apricot jam
    • Blackberry jam
    • Multigrain options

Prices

  • Pizza slices: €3.00
  • Cornetti: €1.50
