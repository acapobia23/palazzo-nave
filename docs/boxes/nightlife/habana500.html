La Habana 500

Habana500 is an authentic Cuban venue officially recognized by the Cuban Embassy in Italy for promoting Cuban culture and friendship between Cubans and Italians.

Located along Lungarno Pecori Giraldi in Florence, right by the Arno River and close to the historic center, it offers a vibrant Latin-inspired riverside experience.

Perfect for sunset aperitifs, summer nights, dancing under the stars, and lively social evenings filled with music, cocktails, and Cuban atmosphere.



Cuban-inspired Food & Tropical Cocktails

Habana500 offers a mix of Cuban-inspired cuisine and relaxed Italian aperitivo culture in a lively riverside setting.

  • Cuban sandwiches and Latin dishes
  • Mojitos, tropical cocktails, and Negronis
  • Aperitifs and sunset drinks by the river
  • Pizza and casual summer dining options
  • Outdoor seating in a summer atmosphere

A colorful and welcoming place where guests can eat, drink, and enjoy the rhythm of Cuba in the heart of Florence.

Events & Entertainment

Habana500 regularly hosts salsa nights, bachata evenings, DJ sets, themed parties, and cultural events during the summer season.

Live Music & Dance Experience

Open-air dance floors and live Latin music create one of Florence's most energetic nightlife experiences by the river.

Experiences & Activities

Guests can join Latin dance classes organized with local schools or enjoy evenings dancing under the stars in a relaxed, social atmosphere.

Practical Tips

  • Free entry
  • Great for groups and social evenings
  • Suitable for beginners and experienced dancers
  • Arrive before sunset for the best atmosphere
  • Check social media for updated events
