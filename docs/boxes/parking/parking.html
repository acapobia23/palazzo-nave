Parking in Florence


Parking in Florence requires some attention, as the historic city center is regulated by the ZTL – Limited Traffic Zone.

Below you will find all the recommended solutions to arrive and park without risking fines.




Private Covered Parking


There are private, guarded, covered parking garages in the city center.
They are generally more expensive, but:

  • They have a ZTL agreement
  • They allow access to the ZTL without purchasing separate permits
  • They offer greater convenience
