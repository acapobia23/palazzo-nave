Orti Dipinti

Orti Dipinti is a contemporary urban garden in the historic center of Florence, created to bring nature back to everyday life. An urban laboratory of sharing, education and beauty: a creative green space for reconnecting with simple experiences, sustainability and community life.

Located inside a former convent garden near Sant'Ambrogio, it offers a completely different atmosphere from the busy tourist areas: green spaces, vegetable gardens and relaxed seating areas.

Perfect for aperitivo, relaxed evenings, hidden local spots, summer events and an alternative Florence atmosphere.
